Frequently Asked Questions


How does AI help business?
AI can help businesses in a lot of aspects - process large amounts of data quickly, give real-time assistance, provide business insights, automate processes, anticipate outcomes, unlock the power of unstructured data, and streamline sales efforts.
What does a machine learning consultant do?
A machine learning consultant helps in developing and adopting the AI/ML algorithm. They also help in understanding the business need, aggregating data, exploring data, building & validating predictive models, and deploying completed models.
May I know your developer's core expertise and industry experience?
Yes. As soon as you hire us as your AI development company, we get the entire team in touch with you, so you understand their skillsets and qualification.
Will I have complete control over the hired developers?
As your project gets started you have the full authority on your hired developers. You can easily access and get in touch with them whenever you want.
What is AI as a service?
Artificial Intelligence as a Service is also called AIaaS which has been outsourced by a third-party vendor. The services may include building and implementing serverless AI applications according to your business requirements.
